EcoTrack User Guide

Understanding your footprint helps you make better choices. Here is how we calculate your impact:

🥩 Beef Meals

What is a serving? One serving is roughly 4-6 ounces (the size of a deck of cards). Beef has a high footprint due to methane emissions and land use.

⚡ Electricity

Where to find kWh? Check your monthly utility bill or "Smart Meter" app. 1 kWh powers a standard LED bulb for about 100 hours.

✈️ Flights

Short vs Long Haul: We categorize "Long Haul" as any flight over 6 hours (e.g., London to NYC). These flights emit significantly more CO2 because of the fuel required for takeoff and high-altitude cruising.
